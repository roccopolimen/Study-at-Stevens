Product and Quotient Rule

Product Rule
> f(x)=a(x)b(x)
f'(x)=a'(x)b(x) + a(x)b'(x)
Quotient Rule
Hint to remember: low di high - high di low over the square of what's below
f(x)=a(x)/b(x)
f'(x) = (b(x)a'(x) - a(x)b'(x))/b(x)^2
