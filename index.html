Welcome to Stevens!
If you have found this webpage, you are probably a first year student here
at Stevens Institute of Technology. Starting college is sometimes a difficult
change for many students. We are here to help. Two classes that are a staple of
just about every computer science major at Stevens, are Introduction to Computer
Science (CS 115) and Calculus (MA 121). This website aims to prepare students to
succeed in all of the topics that will be covered throughout these classes.
Starting off college with a strong foundation in computer science and mathematics
will make a huge impact during your time here at Stevens as these disciplines
typically build off of prerequisite knowledge.
